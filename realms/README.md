# The Realms

## Why the Realms exist

Every Adventure asks different questions.

Some questions are about technology.

Others are about people, decisions, communication or trust.

The Realms exist to make sure no important perspective is overlooked.

They provide a consistent way to explore every Adventure, regardless of the technology involved.

---

## What is a Realm?

A Realm is a way of examining an Adventure.

It does not describe a step in the learning process.

It describes a perspective.

The same Adventure can be viewed through different Realms, each revealing something different.

For example:

- Systems asks what exists.
- Relationships asks how things influence one another.
- Communication asks how information flows.
- Trust asks why the system can be relied upon.

The Adventure remains the same.

The questions change.

---

## Why every Adventure visits every Realm

Every engineering problem involves more than technology.

A system consists of components.

Those components interact.

Information moves between them.

Decisions must be documented.

Solutions must be evaluated.

Trust must be established.

Because of this, every Main Adventure passes through all seven Realms.

Some Realms may play a larger role than others, but none disappear completely.

---

## The Seven Realms

### Systems

Understand the individual parts of a system.

---

### Relationships

Understand how those parts influence one another.

---

### Communication

Understand how information moves through the system.

---

### Trust

Understand why the system can be relied upon.

---

### Expression

Understand how ideas are represented, communicated and documented.

---

### Knowledge

Understand what should be learned, preserved and transferred.

---

### Evaluation

Understand whether the solution achieves its purpose.

---

## Working Together

The Realms are not isolated.

They complement one another.

Looking at an Adventure through multiple Realms leads to a more complete understanding than any single perspective alone.

---

## Looking Ahead

Each Realm has its own guide.

Together they form a shared language that connects every Adventure in the Field Guide.
