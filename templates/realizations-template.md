# Realizations

> *Understanding deepens wonder.*

Realizations capture the ideas that remain valuable after the details have faded.

Unlike Field Notes, they are not tied to a specific implementation.

A Realization should describe a principle, pattern or insight that can be carried into future Adventures.

If a technology changes tomorrow, a good Realization should still be useful.
