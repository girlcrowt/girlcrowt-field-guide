# Adventures

Welcome to the heart of the girlcrowt Field Guide.

This is where ideas become systems.

Every Adventure is a meaningful mission built around a real challenge.

Rather than studying isolated technologies, you'll build something that matters, document the journey, reflect on what you've learned and leave the world richer than you found it.

Every Main Adventure follows the same contract:

```text
Brief
    ↓
Challenge Scenario
    ↓
Success Criteria
    ↓
Training
    ↓
Mission
    ↓
Build
    ↓
Field Notes
    ↓
Realizations
    ↓
World Impact
    ↓
Campfire
```

Each Adventure contributes to a persistent learning world.

Nothing meaningful is ever wasted.

The world remembers.

The explorer becomes.

The expedition starts here.
