## Phase Brief

Installing software does not guarantee that it works as intended.

Before building your Lab, verify that every critical component functions correctly and that the different parts of your environment work together.

The goal of this phase is confidence.

Not confidence based on hope.

Confidence based on evidence.

---

## The Goal

Test your foundation before placing anything important on top of it.

If problems appear now, they are easier to solve than after your Lab has grown.

Finding issues at this stage is a success.

It means the verification process is working.

---

## Suggested Workflow

### Verify the Host

Confirm that the host system is stable and operating as expected.

---

### Verify the Toolkit

Launch every installed tool.

Confirm that it starts correctly and behaves as expected.

---

### Verify Recovery

Review your Recovery Strategy.

Test the recovery mechanisms that are already available.

Confirm that you understand how they work before relying on them.

---

### Verify Documentation

Check that your documentation reflects the current state of your Lab.

Update anything that changed during installation or verification.

---

## Checkpoint

Before continuing to Phase 04, ask yourself:

- Can I explain every part of my current environment?
- Have I verified every critical assumption?
- Do I trust my recovery process?
- Is my documentation up to date?
- Am I building on a stable foundation?

If any answer is "No", resolve it before continuing.

A strong foundation makes every future Adventure easier.

The expedition continues when the foundation can be trusted.
