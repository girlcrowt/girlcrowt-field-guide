# Training

Before every Adventure comes Training.

Training is where tools become familiar.

The Adventure is where those tools become meaningful.

Training is not about memorizing commands.

It is about building confidence before stepping into the unknown.

Mistakes belong here.

Questions belong here.

Curiosity belongs here.

The goal is not perfection.

The goal is readiness.

---

## Training Structure

Every Training module follows the same rhythm.

### Understand

Learn the ideas behind the tool.

### Practice

Use the tool in a safe, focused exercise.

### Prepare

Connect today's lesson to the upcoming Mission.

---

The expedition begins long before the Mission starts.

Training is where explorers prepare their backpack.
