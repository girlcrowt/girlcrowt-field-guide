# Adventure 01

# Build Your First Lab

## Brief

Every explorer needs a place where curiosity is safe.

A place to build.

To break.

To experiment.

To fail.

To understand.

Before we build servers, networks, databases or cloud infrastructure, we first build the environment where learning itself can happen.

This Lab will become the foundation of your persistent learning world.

Every Adventure that follows will return here.

Nothing you build during this Adventure is temporary.

It becomes part of the world.

## Challenge Scenario

You want to learn IT by building real systems.

Experimenting directly on your everyday computer is risky.

Mistakes should be expected.

They should also be reversible.

Before you can explore unknown systems, you need a place where failure is safe and curiosity is encouraged.

Your first mission is to build that place.
